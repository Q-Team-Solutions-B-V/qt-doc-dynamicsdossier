# Dynamics Dossier Documentation

## Overview

**Dynamics Dossier** is a comprehensive dossier management system for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. This powerful extension enables organizations to efficiently manage documents, appointments, tasks, and workflows through centralized dossiers.

## What is Dynamics Dossier?

Dynamics Dossier provides a complete solution for managing various types of dossiers including:

- **Medical Dossiers** - Patient records, medical examinations, treatments  
- **Absence Management** - Sick leave tracking, absence duration monitoring
- **Re-integration Processes** - Return-to-work workflows and procedures
- **Safety Management** - Safety examinations and compliance tracking
- **Document Management** - Centralized document storage and organization
- **Contact Management** - Enhanced contact and person information management

## Key Features

- ✅ **Dossier Management** - Create, manage, and track different types of dossiers
- ✅ **Workflow Automation** - Configurable workflows for various processes
- ✅ **Absence Tracking** - Monitor sick leave and absence durations
- ✅ **Medical Integration** - Medical record management and connections
- ✅ **Contact Extensions** - Enhanced contact and person management
- ✅ **Document Organization** - Centralized document storage per dossier
- ✅ **Task Management** - To-do items and appointment scheduling
- ✅ **Reporting** - Comprehensive reporting and analytics
- ✅ **Multi-language Support** - English and Dutch language support
