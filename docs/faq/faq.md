# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Dynamics Dossier?
**Q**: What exactly is Dynamics Dossier and what does it do?

**A**: Dynamics Dossier is a comprehensive document and case management extension for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. It helps organizations manage dossiers (case files) that contain documents, appointments, tasks, and workflows. It's particularly useful for healthcare inspections, HR management, absence tracking, and any organization that needs to manage structured case files with automated processes.

### Who is Dynamics Dossier designed for?
**Q**: What type of organizations benefit most from Dynamics Dossier?

**A**: Dynamics Dossier is designed for:
- **Healthcare inspection organizations** managing patient files and medical records
- **HR departments** tracking employee absences and medical cases  
- **Legal firms** managing case files and documentation
- **Consulting companies** organizing client projects and documentation
- **Any organization** requiring structured document management with workflow automation

### How is Dynamics Dossier different from standard Business Central document management?
**Q**: What advantages does Dynamics Dossier offer over built-in Business Central features?

**A**: Dynamics Dossier provides:
- **Centralized case management** - All related documents, tasks, and communications in one place
- **Automated workflows** - Predefined processes that trigger based on events and timelines
- **Specialized absence management** - Complete sick leave and return-to-work processes
- **Advanced document organization** - Better categorization and search capabilities
- **Integration capabilities** - APIs for connecting with medical systems and external tools
- **GDPR compliance features** - Built-in privacy controls for sensitive data

## Workflow Questions

### How do workflows work in Dynamics Dossier?
**Q**: Can you explain how the automated workflows function?

**A**: Workflows operate through:
1. **Trigger Events**: Dossier creation, status changes, or date deadlines
2. **Automatic Actions**: Email notifications, task creation, status updates
3. **Time-Based Steps**: Actions scheduled for specific days after trigger events
4. **User Tasks**: Manual steps requiring human intervention or approval
5. **Conditional Logic**: Different paths based on dossier type or other criteria
6. **Progress Tracking**: Complete visibility into workflow execution status

### Can workflows be modified?
**Q**: Can we change the standard workflows to match our processes?

**A**: Yes, workflows are fully customizable:
- **Modify existing workflows** through the workflow setup interface
- **Create custom workflow templates** for specific dossier types
- **Adjust timing** for automatic actions and notifications
- **Add or remove steps** based on your business requirements
- **Configure conditional logic** for different process paths
- **Professional services available** for complex workflow design
